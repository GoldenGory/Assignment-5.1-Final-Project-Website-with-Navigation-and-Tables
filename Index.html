6. beat 2 egg whites until stiff but not dry, then fold them lightly into the batter

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.

2. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, 5 to 7 minutes.

3. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.

4. Combine onion mixture, mashed potatoes, eggs, flour, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl until well blended.

5. Scoop 1/4 to 1/2 cup batter (depending on size of waffle iron) into the center of waffle iron and close lid.

6. Cook until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
